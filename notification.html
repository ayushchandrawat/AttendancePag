Samrat Ashok Technological Institute(SATI)

Internal Smart India Hackathon (SIH)

What it is:
A nationwide initiative by the Indian government to provide students a platform to create innovative solutions to real-world problems faced by society.

SATI's Role:
SATI has participated in the SIH, organizing internal hackathons to select teams to compete in the national event.

Benefits for Students:

  • Develop practical skills
  • Gain national recognition
  • Connect with industry experts

Other Hackathons Organized by SATI

All India Developers Challenge (AIDC):
A significant online event hosted by SATI, bringing together students from across India to collaborate and solve real-world problems using cutting-edge technology.

MSME Idea Hackathon:
SATI has also hosted these hackathons, which focus on generating ideas to benefit the MSME sector and offering funding opportunities from the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises.

How SATI Promotes Hackathons

  • Internal Events: Organizes internal hackathons to identify and groom talented students for national events like SIH.
  • Startup Cell: SATI's Startup Cell actively promotes hackathon-related activities.
  • Focus on Innovation: Fosters a culture of innovation, product development, and problem-solving mindset among students.

For more information, please contact the SATI Startup Cell or visit the official SATI website.
